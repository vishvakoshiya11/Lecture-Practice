🌟 The Tale of the Lost Star

Once upon a time, in a peaceful forest, there lived a tiny magical star. Every night it shined brightly and filled the sky with happiness. One evening, a strong wind carried the little star far away from its home.

The animals searched everywhere for the missing star. The rabbit looked near the river, the owl searched in the trees, and the fox explored the hills. Everyone wanted to bring the star back.

A brave little girl named Lily found the star resting beside a beautiful flower. She carefully picked it up and promised to return it to the sky. The star smiled with joy and thanked Lily for her kindness.

That night, Lily climbed the highest hill and gently released the star. It flew back into the sky, shining brighter than ever before. The whole village celebrated the return of the lost star.

Moral: Kindness and courage can bring light to everyone's life.
